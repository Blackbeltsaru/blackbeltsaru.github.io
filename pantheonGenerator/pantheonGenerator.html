Pantheon Generator

This is a small project that is able to generate a pantheon of gods that pertain to specific domains and alignments. The project makes use of markov chains in order to build the diety names. The domains are pseudo randomly assigned to dieties by generating "realms" to which the dieties belong and picking domains from those realms.

The match length of the markov chain as well as the number of dieties generated can be customized below.
